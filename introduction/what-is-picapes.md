# Introduction

**PiCapes** makes custom Minecraft capes simple and accessible for everyone. It works across **all Minecraft versions** and **account types**, giving you a smooth and reliable cape experience.

PiCapes supports **Fabric 1.21.8+** through the **Cape Provider X** mod, along with clients and mods that display **OptiFine capes**, including **1.8 clients with OptiFine**, **OptiFine mod**, **Capes mod**, and more.
